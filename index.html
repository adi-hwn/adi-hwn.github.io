Titration Curve Software

A friend and I worked together on a research project for OPSI (Olimpiade Penelitian Siswa Indonesia/Indonesian Students' Research Olympiad). We derived an iterative formula that could accurately calculate the pH value of a given acid - base mixture. Based on that formula, I coded a JavaFX application that could compute the pH and draw titration curves based on it. Screen shot of the program in action. I coded it in 2013, meaning newer versions of JavaFX no longer work well with it. The curve (of volume (mL) vs pH) still seems to work.
